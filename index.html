Djamal Zerrouki is a Professor of Chemical Engineering at the University of Ouargla, He holds a Ph.D. (2008) from the University of Paris (UPMC) and M.Sc. (2004) in Surface and interfacial science from the University of Paris-Diderot, France and Engineer's degree in Industrial Maintenance Engineering from the University of Boumerdes, Algeria (2002). Before his current appointment, he served as CSO and cofounder of PhotoFuel, Project Manager Capsum, biomethods SA and Postdoctoral Research Associate at LCMD ESPCI. He has held short-term research visits at Chengdu University, China,(2008),

His research lies at the intersection of “Process Engineering”, and “Materials Science” which combines fundamental science and engineering principles to arrive at solutions for real-world applications, his interests include Colloids and Formulation Engineering. Formulation, surfactant, emulsions, foams, dispersions, nanoparticle synthesis. Bioprocess engineering and its environmental applications. Biological Waste Treatment: performance and design of biological treatment systems. Microalgae production from carbon dioxide, sunlight, and wastewater for the production of biofuels and biochemicals. Biofilm formation and adhesion. Microbiology and floc formation in waste treatment systems .
