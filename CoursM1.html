Overview

The main objectiveThe main objective of this graduate-level course is to provide an in-depth understanding of the porosity concepts and give a general overview on the porous media characterization . The course is organized in three parts: (i) introduction; (ii) flow on the porous media (iii) unique particle motion, fluidization and filtreation .

Content

A porous medium is a solid matrix that is partially filled by interconnected voids (pores) which can convey fluids under an applied pressure gradient. The simplest characterization of the geometry of pores is in terms of two parameters: the porosity n and the specific surface s. The first one is defined as the ratio between the void and the total volume of an elementary volume of the medium that contains a large number of pores.
